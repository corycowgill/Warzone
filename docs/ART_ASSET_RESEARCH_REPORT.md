## Executive Summary

After searching poly.pizza, Sketchfab, Kenney.nl, Quaternius, OpenGameArt, itch.io, TurboSquid, CGTrader, Free3D, and GitHub, the following key findings emerged:

**Best Overall Packs for This Project:**
1. **ITHappy Studios Military FREE** - 26 military-specific models (tanks, barrels, tower, tent, radio, hedgehog, mortar) in GLTF
2. **Zsky Military Base Pack** (itch.io) - 20+ military buildings (barracks, med tent, sniper tower) CC-BY 4.0
3. **Sketchfab Military Buildings Modular Kit** by hamzaunity3d - 13 modular pieces, free CC-BY
4. **Quaternius** via Poly Pizza - Barracks, Watch Tower, Town Center, and 1400+ models in CC0
5. **Kenney Packs** (already in project) - Tower Defense Kit, Castle Kit, Nature Kit all CC0

**Critical Gaps in Current Assets:**
- No dedicated military HQ/command post (using fantasy fortress)
- No quonset hut / warehouse for Supply Depot
- No airfield/hangar with military look (using space-kit hangars)
- No sandbag walls, barbed wire, tank traps
- No V2 rocket / superweapon model (using tower defense piece)
- No AA gun emplacement (using tower defense cannon)
- No military radar/radio antenna
