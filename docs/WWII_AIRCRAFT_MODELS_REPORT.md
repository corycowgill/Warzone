# WWII Aircraft 3D Model Research Report

> Research conducted April 2026 for Warzone RTS project.
> Target: Low-poly stylized models, 300-2,000 triangles preferred (per ART_ASSET_MANIFEST.md).
> Preferred formats: GLTF/GLB (Three.js compatible).

---

## Summary of Findings

After searching poly.pizza, sketchfab.com, kenney.nl, quaternius.com, opengameart.org, itch.io, turbosquid.com, cgtrader.com, free3d.com, thingiverse.com, archive3d.net, clara.io, and GitHub repositories, the results are organized below by aircraft category.

**Key takeaway:** The single best resource for this project is **manilov.ap on Sketchfab** -- a creator who has published dozens of free CC-BY military aircraft models in a consistent low-poly style, covering P-47, Yak-3, Ju-52, C-47, and B-17. Combined with a few other Sketchfab creators for Spitfire, Bf 109, Zero, and bombers, you can assemble a complete WWII air force roster entirely from free downloads.

## DECIMATION PIPELINE

Most models found exceed the project's 300-2,000 triangle budget. Recommended pipeline:

1. Download GLTF from Sketchfab
2. Import into Blender
3. Apply Decimate modifier (ratio ~0.1-0.3 depending on source poly count)
4. Target: 1,000-2,000 tris per aircraft
5. Bake simplified materials to vertex colors or single texture atlas
6. Export as GLB
7. Test in Three.js scene at game-appropriate scale
